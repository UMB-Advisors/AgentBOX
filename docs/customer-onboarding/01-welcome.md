## Common questions

**Do I need to know anything technical?** No. Every step is point-and-click, and this guide walks through each screen. The only things you'll need are the password you want to use and the Google account for the inbox you want managed.

**How long does this take?** About ten minutes start to finish. Most of that is the Gmail connection in Step 5.

**Can I do this on my phone?** Yes. The wizard works on a phone, tablet, or laptop. A laptop is a little easier for the Gmail consent screen in Step 5, but a phone is fine.

## What to do if it fails

**The welcome screen won't load at all.** You typed the appliance's web address and got an error, a security warning, or a blank page.

1. Double-check the address. It should be the exact web address you were given (for example `https://mailbox.yourcompany.com`), including the `https://`.
2. Confirm the appliance is powered on and the status light is on. Give it two minutes after first plug-in — it needs a moment to come online and request its security certificate.
3. If your browser shows a certificate or "not secure" warning, the appliance may still be setting up its certificate. Wait two minutes and reload. If it persists past five minutes, contact support — the certificate may not have been issued yet.
4. If the page still won't load, confirm your device is on the same network as the appliance (or connected through the address support gave you), then reload.

If none of that works, send the exact error message to support and we'll take it from there.
