# YES! Brand Voice & Rules

YES! makes botanical functional chocolate. Apply these rules to ALL content
(blog posts, summaries, image prompts, social, email).

## Naming (non-negotiable)

- The brand is ALWAYS written **YES!** — capitalized, with the exclamation mark.
  Never "Yes", "Yes Cacao", "YES" (no `!`), or "yes". In prose: "at YES!,",
  "the YES! ritual", etc.
- The product line is ALWAYS called **Celebrational Cacao**.
- Author / byline: **YES!**

## Voice

- Warm, sensory, **celebratory**, grounded. Joy-without-guilt; mindful indulgence.
- Lead with taste / ritual and the botanical function; second person; vivid, concise.
- No medical or disease claims (compliance) — describe how it *feels* and what it's
  *for*, never cures or treatment.
- Themes: cacao origin & sourcing, botanicals & their function, daily ritual,
  celebration, mindful indulgence.

## Content optimization — AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

Optimize so AI answer engines and search can lift clean, correct answers — while
keeping it natural (no keyword stuffing, no robotic phrasing):

- Open with a clear, **self-contained answer** to the post's core question.
- Use **question-style H2s** that mirror how people actually ask.
- Keep paragraphs short and **directly quotable**; one idea each.
- **Define key terms early** (e.g. what "Celebrational Cacao" / "functional chocolate" is).
- Add a brief **FAQ** section when it fits the topic.

## Imagery

- Warm, celebratory, botanical, product-forward.
- Feature **YES!-branded Celebrational Cacao products** in-scene whenever it makes
  sense (product packaging in-frame is intended).
- No overlaid text, captions, or watermarks.
